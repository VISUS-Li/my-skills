# Excluded Scope

Version 1 focuses on pure-code knowledge videos and director orchestration.

## Do Not Implement By Default

- Black-box text-to-video models such as Seedance, Kling, Veo, Runway, Sora, Higgsfield, Pexo, or inference.sh video generation.
- Raw footage heavy editing.
- Complex talking-head recuts.
- Automated social publishing to Douyin, Bilibili, X, YouTube, or other platforms.
- Automatic scraping or downloading third-party video assets.
- Copyright-unclear asset use.
- One-click black-box final videos without storyboard and QA review.
- Bulk copying external repository docs or source into this skill.

## Future Extensions

Keep these as future integrations, not v1 requirements:

- TTS provider integration.
- Foley or SFX generation.
- Raw footage editing.
- AI video model B-roll.
- Social publishing.
- Analytics-driven iteration.
- Deep Codex Storyboard integration.
- Remotion Studio review automation.

If the user explicitly asks for an excluded capability, state the tradeoff, keep storyboard and QA artifacts, and route to the appropriate specialized skill or tool.
