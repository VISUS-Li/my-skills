# Sound Style Recipes

Use these with `visual-style-recipes.md`.

## Premium tech comparison
- Music: minimal electronic pulse, clean sub, sparse plucks, no vocals.
- SFX: data ticks, UI clicks, soft digital whooshes, controlled impacts.
- Ambience: low digital room tone or subtle server hum.
- Avoid: loud trailer booms on small UI movements.

## Douyin high-energy explainer
- Music: fast pulse or trend-like instrumental, strong first 3 seconds.
- SFX: subtitle pops, swishes, notification taps, beat-synced impacts.
- Technique: dense hook, then reduce under explanatory sections.
- Avoid: every word popping; voice must stay clear.

## Enterprise data report
- Music: calm confident corporate bed, restrained rhythm.
- SFX: soft number ticks, chart lockups, subtle page/slide transitions.
- Ambience: polished office/boardroom silence, not noisy.
- Avoid: playful cartoon UI sounds.

## Food / spicy snack product promo
- Music: energetic, warm, percussive.
- SFX/Foley: package crinkle, crunch, sizzle, spice sprinkle, heat whoosh, product pop.
- Ambience: kitchen/street-food texture if relevant.
- Avoid: fake over-the-top crunch if no visual bite exists.

## Logo particle animation
- Music: short riser or tonal bed.
- SFX: particle shimmer, reverse swell, logo impact, soft tail.
- Ambience: none or very subtle.
- Avoid: multiple impacts after logo has already settled.

## Song-dynasty guofeng history / AI development
- Music: guqin/xiao-like texture or modern ambient with Chinese instrumentation cues.
- SFX: brush stroke, paper rustle, water ripple, seal stamp, soft gong.
- Ambience: mountain wind, paper room tone.
- Avoid: stock martial-arts whooshes unless the scene language is action-oriented.

## Health proverb / biological science
- Music: warm calm marimba/piano/ambient pulse.
- SFX: soft heartbeat, liquid drops, microscope click, organism movement, gentle data tick.
- Ambience: lab/clinic calm bed.
- Avoid: alarming medical beeps unless communicating danger.
