# Visual Style Recipes

Use a recipe as a starting point, then adapt it to the user's brand, topic, and platform.

## Premium tech comparison

- Palette: midnight/navy base, violet or cyan primary, amber accent, low-saturation surfaces.
- Typography: bold geometric sans, numeric emphasis.
- Composition: split screens, system diagrams, bento grids, data cards, glowing connectors.
- Motion: slow push, parallax stack, data morph, mask reveal, subtle particles.
- Assets: product icons, UI mockups, screenshots, code snippets, data marks.
- Avoid: random neon overload, too many gradients, centered bullet cards.

## Douyin high-energy explainer

- Palette: high-contrast dark/light base with one aggressive accent.
- Typography: large kinetic headlines, karaoke captions, emoji/icons only if intentional.
- Composition: fast inserts, crop jumps, big numbers, bold outlines.
- Motion: snap zoom, whip pan, bounce emphasis, rhythmic subtitle pops.
- Assets: reaction clips, UI crops, icons, sound hits.
- Avoid: long static explanations, tiny text, no hook in first 3 seconds.

## Enterprise data report

- Palette: clean dark or white base, corporate blue/green, restrained accent.
- Typography: clear hierarchy, numeric emphasis, source labels.
- Composition: dashboards, chart builds, table-to-insight transformations, executive summary cards.
- Motion: calm data reveal, no excessive bounce, smooth transitions.
- Assets: charts, maps, logos if allowed, report/document crops.
- Avoid: flashy effects that reduce trust.

## Food / spicy snack product promo

- Palette: red/orange/yellow heat, dark contrast, fresh green or white highlights.
- Typography: bold appetite-driven display; labels like texture, crunch, spice.
- Composition: macro close-ups, ingredient orbit, packaging hero, heat wave, bite moment.
- Motion: quick cuts, steam/particles, impact hits, sauce/seasoning transitions.
- Assets: product pack, ingredient images, texture plates, SFX crunch, flame/steam.
- Avoid: sterile corporate layouts, generic abstract icons.

## Logo particle animation

- Palette: brand colors with dark/clean base.
- Typography: minimal; let logo lead.
- Composition: particles gather, scatter, orbit, resolve into mark.
- Motion: force fields, trails, slow reveal, final settle.
- Assets: vector logo, particle system, shimmer texture, brand sound mark.
- Avoid: overcomplicated backgrounds competing with logo.

## Song-dynasty guofeng history / AI development

- Palette: rice paper, ink black, mineral green/blue, cinnabar, muted gold.
- Typography: modern readable CJK plus calligraphic accents.
- Composition: scroll timeline, misty mountain depth, ink washes, seals, map routes.
- Motion: ink bloom, scroll pan, slow drift, brush mask wipe.
- Assets: landscape plates, seal icons, paper texture, ink lines, historical diagrams.
- Avoid: fake unreadable calligraphy as body text, random temple imagery.

## Health proverb / biological science

- Palette: clean medical white/dark, teal/blue/green, warm human accent.
- Typography: calm, legible, approachable.
- Composition: proverb card → body system diagram → micro insert → practical takeaway.
- Motion: microscope zoom, cell/particle flow, line animation, gentle emphasis.
- Assets: anatomical icons, cell textures, diagram labels, real-life behavior images.
- Avoid: fear-mongering visuals unless medically justified.

## Editorial documentary explainer

- Palette: neutral base, warm paper/cream or documentary black, one accent.
- Typography: magazine-style headline, lower thirds, source tags.
- Composition: archival image crop, quote card, map, timeline, talking head, B-roll.
- Motion: slow pan/zoom, cut on action, subtle overlays.
- Assets: licensed/public-domain images, documents, maps, human footage.
- Avoid: overly synthetic look when credibility matters.
