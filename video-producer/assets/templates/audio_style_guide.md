## Mix priorities
1. Dialogue / voiceover intelligibility
2. Important narrative SFX and Foley
3. Music groove and emotional bed
4. Ambience and decorative texture

## Default level guide
- Voiceover peak target: -6 dBFS to -3 dBFS before final loudness pass
- Music under narration: about -22 to -16 dB relative to full-scale source, then adjust by ear
- SFX under narration: short accents, usually -18 to -8 dB depending on narrative importance
- Ambient beds: subtle, usually -30 to -22 dB
- Final true peak: <= -1.0 dBTP unless platform profile says otherwise
