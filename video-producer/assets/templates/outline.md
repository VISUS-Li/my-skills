## Oral delivery notes
- Bridge lines must **cite or point to evidence** (原文/条款/时间线), not speculative chat
- Allowed rhythm: `[pause]`, ……, repeat key fact words, “不对，更准确地说…”
- Forbidden: 你想啊 / 其实吧 / 我跟你说 / 无来源的我觉得估计
